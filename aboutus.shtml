Five Springs farm is owned and operated by
Mason and Lisa Chambers. They founded the nursery in 1995,
and have operated it continuously since. It is located at
12691 Dutch Creek Road not far from Peach Ridge Road.
 
The nursery offers a wide range of plants, specializing in
natives, pawpaws, magnolias, buckeyes, sedums, bamboo, grasses,
and ferns. We have over two dozen cultivars of Japanese maples
and around 30 species of hardy ferns.
We also have great pollinator plants. We also have a few
very rare plants that only we sell.

Directions to the nursery, coming from Athens

  1. Take US-33 to the Columbus Road / OH-550 / OH-13 exit, which is the first exit west of East State Street on the east side of Athens or the first exit east of Johnson Road in The Plains.
  2. Take the exit. If coming from the west, turn left onto OH-550. If coming from the east, turn right onto OH-550 at the light.
  3. Go 4.5 miles to the top of the dividing ridge. This is the only ridge you'll go over between Athens and Sharpsburg, so it's unmistakeable.
  4. Turn right on top of the dividing ridge onto Peach Ridge Road / Dutch Creek Road. You'll need to take this turn very slowly, and you'll go up very steeply at first.
  5. At the stop sign at the top of the hill, bear left onto Dutch Creek Road. This is a four-way intersection, with a driveway to the left (on the ridgetop), Peach Ridge Road to the right (on the ridgetop), and Dutch Creek Road ahead, slightly to the left, going down into the valley.
  6. At 0.5 mile from OH-550, turn into the first driveway to the right. Warning: This is a sharp turn. You're now at the nursery.
